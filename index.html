About Ram Ratan & Co.

Our Vision

To be a catalyst for agricultural innovation by developing high-quality, cost-effective research solutions that empower sustainable farming and contribute meaningfully to the advancement of agriculture in the developing world.

Who We Are

Established in 2025, Ram Ratan & Company (RRC) is a specialty materials company delivering innovative solutions for agricultural research and seed testing. We are pioneers in the manufacturing of pollination bags, seed packets, plant labels, germination papers, and are recognized quality leaders in nylon cage nets, specialized plasticware, and other niche agricultural products.

In addition, we are well equipped to support agricultural seed companies with end-to-end consultancy services covering regulatory compliance and liaison activities. Our expertise includes in-house R&D unit recognition and renewal with DSIR, export and import of seeds, overseas germplasm import, Plant Variety Protection (PVP) and seed licensing services.

We serve many of India’s leading seed companies, seed testing laboratories, and agricultural institutions, while also catering to select adjacent markets. Headquartered in New Delhi, RRC has a presence across more than 30 distribution points in India, supported by a growing network of distribution partners across the EMEA and APAC regions. This extensive distribution network enables timely delivery of our solutions, supporting customers in their time-sensitive research activities.

Our Team

Our management and advisory teams bring together professionals from diverse backgrounds, enabling us to offer comprehensive and innovative solutions for agricultural research and testing. The team combines expertise across agricultural research, farm operations, manufacturing, supply chain management, and quality assurance, ensuring a well-rounded approach to problem-solving.

The promoters have deep-rooted experience within the agricultural research ecosystem, with long-standing associations across seed companies, research institutions, and testing laboratories. This strong industry linkage allows the leadership to anticipate evolving research needs, understand field-level challenges, and translate them into practical, reliable, and scalable solutions.

Their collective insight and hands-on experience guide the company’s strategic direction, reinforcing our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer-centricity in agricultural research and testing.

Founder's Story

Ram Ratan & Company was founded by Mr. Yogendra Gupta, a seasoned professional with over 28 years of experience in the agricultural research industry, including tenures at Sungro Seeds Pvt. Ltd. and Mahyco Pvt. Ltd. Through his extensive industry exposure, he identified the lack of reliable, locally sourced quality materials as a critical challenge in agricultural research and testing. This insight led to the establishment of RRC with a vision to build a comprehensive ecosystem for the development and supply of such specialty materials.

Today, RRC offers products developed specifically for the Indian market that rival global benchmarks, establishing the company as a market leader in specialty materials for agricultural research and seed testing.

Quality Standard

Ram Ratan & Co. places a strong emphasis on robust processes and stringent quality control to consistently deliver high-quality products and services. Our manufacturing units are equipped with best-in-class machinery and skilled workers, ensuring excellence across product development, manufacturing, and quality assurance for all materials.

Cross-border business growth can be transformative; however, managing the complexities of international shipping is far from simple. Regulatory compliance, logistics coordination, and customs clearance are among the many challenges businesses commonly face. This is where our specialized professional team adds value. We deliver comprehensive import and export documentation services for seed companies across India, supported by expert export–import consulting to streamline the entire process and ensure smooth, efficient, and compliant international trade.

LIAISON & REGULATORY CONSULTANCY SERVICES

Import of Germplasm from Overseas Institutions:

With over three decades of experience in senior R&D roles at leading seed companies, our professional team delivers end-to-end solutions for importing germplasm for research purposes from overseas institutions. We facilitate the release of imported consignments through close coordination with the National Bureau of Plant Genetic Resources (NBPGR), India, and international institutions. In addition, we provide professional services to seed companies across India for obtaining certification of Post-Entry Quarantine (PEQ) facilities from NBPGR for the cultivation of imported germplasm.

Recognition and Renewal of In-house R&D Units from DSIR:

With advisors who have extensive experience in seed companies and research institutions, we assist agriculture-based organizations in securing recognition of their R&D centres from the Department of Scientific & Industrial Research (DSIR), Government of India. We prepare and submit applications with speed and utmost accuracy, based on the information provided by our clients. Our turnkey solutions cater to a diverse client base across the seed industry. With a high success rate, our expertise is well established and trusted. Headquartered in Delhi, we proudly serve clients across India.

Plant Variety Protection related services:
