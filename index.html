About Ram Ratan & Co.

Established in 2025, Ram Ratan & Company (RRC) is a pioneer in manufacturing specialty materials for agricultural research and seed testing. We serve India's leading seed companies and institutions.

Founded by Mr. Yogendra Gupta, who brings over 28 years of industry experience (including tenures at Sungro Seeds & Mahyco), we bridge the gap for reliable, locally sourced quality materials.
